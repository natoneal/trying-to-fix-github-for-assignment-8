Meet evo

evo is an app designed around the needs of William, a special needs educator.

opportunity space:

After conducting research and narrowing down our opportunity spaces, we identified the key solution William wants: to have an easy way to get his students out in the real world more often. We want to solve this through creating accessible technology for him in the form of an application.

solution space:

William wants his students to get out in the real world more often. We want to solve this problem through creating accessible technology in the form of a specialized graphic user interface made just for William. evo addresses Williamâ€™s opportunity space through:

Making it easy to visually search for accessible locations for school trips within the Savannah, GA area

Scheduling school field trips for him using Google Duplex

Keeping a timeline memoria of past field trips and review how the trip went

Tracking students with GPS tags in case of emergency on the trip

Making it easy to sort, upload, and share photographs of the trip with parents
